About

I'm a maintainer of Compute.Studio and ParamTools. I also contribute to a number of other projects in the PSLmodels ecosystem. Growing up, I wanted to be an economist, but I also had an unexplained attraction to Wired magazine. In college, I discovered that this attraction stemmed from a latent desire to be a software developer. I've gone back and forth between these two career paths ever since. These days, I'm on a long swing towards becoming a software developer.

I've created this site to give myself a place to write about software, bikes, and books.
